Our History

TechCon started in 2010 as a small developer meetup and has grown into one of the leading international conferences for tech innovators, developers, and entrepreneurs.

Our Mission

TechCon's mission is to inspire innovation, promote collaboration, and bring together technology leaders from around the globe to share knowledge and build the future.

Notable Past Speakers

Over the years, TechCon has hosted a variety of thought leaders and trailblazers in the tech industry.

Dr. Ada Lovelace

Renowned computer scientist known for her contributions to early programming concepts.

Elon Tusk

Visionary entrepreneur who shared insights on sustainable technology and space innovation.

Grace Hopper

Pioneer in computer programming and U.S. Navy rear admiral who helped develop early compilers.
